Our Story

A Huntington
Institution

Hoosier Drive-In has been a staple of Huntington for over six decades. When locals want a real breaded tenderloin, loaded Hoosier fries, or a thick hand-dipped milkshake — this is where they come. No gimmicks, no shortcuts. Just classic Indiana comfort food done right.
